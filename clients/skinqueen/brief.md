# SkinQueens — Agent Brief

**App:** AI skincare that tells you if products match YOUR specific skin (Skin DNA profile).
**Tagline:** "Stop guessing. Start matching."
**Core mechanic:** Share a TikTok video → app extracts every product mentioned → rates each Gold / Rose / Stone for YOUR skin. The screen-record of this flow IS the content.

## What creators post about

1. **Share-to-app flow** — Watch skincare TikTok, share to SkinQueens, react to the verdicts. This is the #1 hook.
2. **Skin DNA Quiz reveal** — 22 questions, ~2 min, live on camera. React to your archetype result.
3. **Skincare drawer audit** — Show existing products, scan each one, react to what works and what doesn't.
4. **Product conflict reveal** — Routine Grader shows which products cancel each other out. Common: retinol + AHA, vitamin C + benzoyl peroxide.
5. **Dupe finder** — Cheaper alternative with same actives as the $80 product they were about to buy.

## Content rules (DO)
- Show the app being USED — not just the icon or splash screen
- Film genuine reactions to quiz results and product verdicts
- Name of app must be visible on screen during use
- Mention the app name naturally in description (no direct CTA needed)

## Content rules (DON'T)
- Never add "download now" or "link in bio" directly in the video
- Never script the reaction — authentic surprise converts, fake doesn't
- Never make medical claims ("this will cure your acne")
- Never promise specific skin results
