### 2.1 ONSKIN

**Website:** onskin.com  
**Company:** AIBY Inc. — Miami, FL  
**App Store Category:** Education, Science & Reference  

#### Product
OnSkin is a cosmetic ingredient scanner. The user scans a barcode, takes a photo of the product, or searches by name. The app returns:
- **Safety Score** (0-100): based on hormonal risk, carcinogens, allergens according to international databases
- **Skin Match**: whether the product is ideal, potential, or not for your skin type
- **Alternative recommendations** when the score is low

**Key differentiators:**
- Database of 2M+ products
- 100% ad-free, no brand affiliations
- "Safe Choice" badge for products scoring 95+
- Partner of the Skin Cancer Foundation
- Won Webby Award 2024 (double: Webby Winner + People's Voice)
- Team of medical experts evaluates each ingredient

**Data:**
- 8M+ users
- Rating: 4.7
- TikTok content organized in playlists: Cosmetics, Makeup, Safety Scores, Hacks, Skincare guide, DIY, Types of beauty, Advent Calendars, Acne, Hair style, Tips, Skincare evolution, Ingredients

**Pricing:**
- Free (no subscription visible on website)
- No ads or brand affiliations
- Revenue model: likely freemium with premium features in-app

#### UGC/Content Strategy
OnSkin uses TikTok as its main organic platform. It has **944.2K likes with only 11.3K followers** — an extraordinarily high ratio (83:1 likes/follower). A signal that its content has a very strong hook rate and retention.

**Observed UGC formats:**
| Format | Description | Engagement |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Safety Score Reveal | "Scanning my bathroom products" — barcode by barcode | High |
| Comparison format | "Safe vs Unsafe" side by side | High |
| "Clean beauty check" | Full routine walk-through with scores | Medium-high |
| Ingredient breakdown | Deep-dive on a controversial ingredient | High |
| Hack format | "You didn't know this about your sunscreen" | High |
| Brand comparison | Same category, different scores | Very high |

**Most common hooks in OnSkin UGC:**
- "What is REALLY in your [product]?" (curiosity + fear)
- "I gave my skincare routine a safety score and I was speechless"
- "POV: You scan your products and discover the most expensive one has a score of 43"
- "[Viral product] has this in its ingredients — score: BAD"
- "Finally an app that tells you the truth about your skincare"
- Real-time safety reveal (ASMR of the scan + suspense before the score)

### 2.2 LÓVI

**Website:** lovi.care  
**Company:** Digital Skincare — Cyprus  
**Tagline:** "Science-backed AI Cosmetologist you can trust"  

#### Product
Lóvi is the most feature-complete competitor. It has 3 "superpowers":

1. **Face Scanner** — Detects 11+ skin issues (post-acne spots, wrinkles, acne inflammatory, whiteheads, redness, pigmentation, scars, visible pores, moles, nevi, freckles). Tracks changes over time with monthly history.

2. **Product Scanner with Fit Score** — Scans without a barcode (direct photo). Gives a personalized score (not generic like OnSkin). Tells you if the product works for YOUR specific skin ID. Not affiliated with any brand.

3. **24/7 AI Cosmetologist** — AI chat trained on medical literature (PubMed, BioLink, BERT). Notably: **"Lóvi AI surpassed the pass mark on US Medical License Exam"**. Answers questions like "What are the top 3 SPFs for my skin?" with personalized recommendations + prices.

**Medical board:** 7 experts — dermatologists, cosmetic chemists, aesthetic practitioners.

**Key differentiators:**
- Face scanner with progress tracking (unique vs competitors)
- AI that passed the US medical license exam
- Completely independent from brands
- Won Beauty Innovation Award
- "Skincare that feels like self care" — emotional positioning

**Social data:**
- TikTok @lovibeautyworld: 3.3K followers, 18.9K likes
- Instagram @lovibeautyworld: 43.5K followers
- Facebook, YouTube, Snapchat, LinkedIn, Pinterest — multi-channel presence

**Pricing:**
- Subscription (has "money-back policy" — sign of a paywall)
- Specific model not openly published on web (likely revealed in-app)
- Estimated: ~$7-15/month or ~$40-70/year based on comparable market

#### UGC/Content Strategy
Lóvi is the app with the best UGC on Instagram (43.5K) but relatively low on TikTok. Its highest-engagement videos combine: technical demonstration + emotional result.

**Observed UGC formats:**

**Most common hooks in Lóvi UGC:**

### 2.3 SKINSORT

**Website:** skinsort.com  
**Implicit tagline:** "Know exactly which products are right for you"  

#### Product
SkinSort is the closest thing to a database/research tool rather than a "lifestyle app". It's web-first, very strong in SEO. Millions of users but almost no active social presence.

**Features:**
- Search by product or ingredient
- **Personalized Score** based on skin type, concerns, and history
- Easy-to-understand ingredient breakdown (pros, cons, safety)
- **Dupe Finder** — finds cheaper equivalent alternatives
- **Side-by-side comparison** between two products
- Routine builder with conflict detection
- Shelves (organize products: "I have", "want to try", etc.)
- Authentic reviews by skin type similar to yours
- Advanced filters (fungal acne safe, hormonal acne, rosacea, etc.)
- Up-to-date on reformulations

**Key differentiators:**
- Best dupe finder on the market (killer feature)
- Side-by-side comparison tool (unique)
- Very strong SEO — appears when someone googles "X ingredients"
- Community of reviews by skin concern
- Independent from brands, privacy-first

**Social data:**
- TikTok @skinsort: **479 followers, 656 likes** — practically nonexistent
- Instagram @skinsort: **2,864 followers, 69 posts**
- But: **1,100+ Reels on Instagram about SkinSort** from other users
- Millions of organic users (SEO-driven, not social-driven)
- Indonesian creator @devitainggrit has a video about SkinSort with **658K likes**

**Pricing:**
- Free (current main model)
- Possible Pro tier in development (advanced features)

#### UGC/Content Strategy
SkinSort generates massive organic UGC **without investing in creators**. Their community uses it as a research tool and shares naturally. 90% of the content is user-initiated, not paid.

**Observed UGC formats:**

| Product comparison | "SkinSort says: this one wins because of these ingredients" | Medium |
| Brand callout | "Brand X has a low score on SkinSort" | High |

**Most common hooks in SkinSort UGC:**
- "Save the name of this app: SKINSORT — thank me later" (viral, 658K)
- "Must have app if you love skincare"
- "SkinSort is a life saver and money saver"

## 4. HOOK AND UGC FORMAT ANALYSIS BY APP

### Hook categories that perform best (observed across all apps)

**Tier 1 — Highest-performing hooks:**
1. **"Save the name of this app"** — curiosity + FOMO + utility
2. **Real-time score reveal** — suspense + surprise + authentic reaction
3. **"This saved my skin / saved me money"** — transformation + social proof
4. **Viral product callout** — "[Product everyone uses] has a [bad/good] score — let me explain"
5. **Dupe reveal** — "The dupe of [$90 product] exists and it's in this app"

**Tier 2 — Educational hooks:**
6. **"If you know this, your skincare is ahead"** — aspirational + intelligence
7. **"The number 1 mistake you make buying skincare"** — fear/guilt hook
8. **"Did you know [ingredient X] does this?"** — curiosity + science
9. **"My dermatologist told me this and I confirmed it with [app]"** — authority

**Tier 3 — Lifestyle hooks:**
10. **"POV: your routine before/after discovering [app]"** — transformation
11. **"I've been following this routine since using [app] and my skin..."** — before/after
12. **Face scan reveal** — "This is what the scan found on my face" (Lóvi only)

## 6. GAPS AND OPPORTUNITIES FOR SKINSQUEEN UGC

### What SkinQueen has that no competitor has:
1. **TikTok video scan** — absolutely unique, no one else does it
2. **9 Skin Archetypes** — shareable identity, enormous viral potential
3. **22-question Skin DNA** — perception of greater personalization
4. **Entry price** — $9.99/month vs what a derm visit costs

### Gaps vs competitors that affect UGC:
| Gap | Competitor that resolves it | UGC impact |
|-----|----------------------------|-----------|
| No face scanner | Lóvi has it | Lóvi generates unique visual "reveal" |
| No AI chat | Lóvi has it | Lóvi has conversational UGC |

| No global safety score | OnSkin has it | OnSkin has fear/surprise hook |
| No community reviews | SkinSort has it | SkinSort has peer testimonials |

### Recommended UGC formats for SkinQueen (based on the benchmark):

**Highest-potential hook:**
> "The app that analyzes the TikTok videos you saved to tell you if the products work for YOUR skin"

**Top 5 formats to test:**

1. **"Before spending a dime on skincare, I do this first"**
   - Format: Screen recording of the TikTok video scan in real time
   - Hook: Scan a viral product video → show if it's a match or not for their skin
   - Duration: 20-30s

2. **"What Skin Archetype are you?"**
   - Format: Personal archetype reveal → stitch/response invitation

3. **"I spent $[X] on products that are wrong for my skin"**
   - Format: Old haul → run each product through SkinQueen → discover mismatches
   - Hook: Relatable frustration + SkinQueen as the solution
   - Duration: 30-60s

4. **"This product has [X] million views on TikTok — SkinQueen says…"**
   - Format: Take a trending product, scan it, show match/mismatch
   - Hook: Cultural relevance + result surprise
   - Duration: 20-30s

5. **"My Skin DNA says I'm [Archetype] and this is what I changed"**
   - Format: Transformation testimonial with concrete Skin DNA test data
   - Hook: Credibility + identity + visual result
   - Duration: 45-60s

## 8. RECOMMENDATIONS FOR SKINSQUEEN UGC BRIEFS
