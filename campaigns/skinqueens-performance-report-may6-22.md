## Why the Numbers Grow Next Week

The campaign model requires each creator to open **new dedicated accounts** for skincare content and warm them up for a minimum of 3 days before publishing. This maximizes organic reach on TikTok — a new account trained on the SkinTok algorithm gets better distribution than a mixed personal account.

That process took the first week. The second week (this one) is when the accounts are ready. The third week (starting now) is when real volume kicks in.
