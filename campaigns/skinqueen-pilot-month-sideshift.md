### Product / App Description

```
SkinQueens is an AI skincare app that tells you if products actually work for 
YOUR skin — not just any skin.

How it works: you take a 22-question Skin DNA Quiz that builds your personal 
skin profile (type, sensitivities, concerns, climate, budget). The app then 
matches every product to your profile with a score: Gold (great match), 
Rose (decent match), or Stone (skip it).

Key features to show in your content:
• Share-to-App Flow: Watch a skincare video on TikTok → share it to SkinQueens 
  → the app extracts every product mentioned and tells you if it matches your 
  skin. The screen-record of this flow IS the content.
• Skin DNA Quiz: 22 questions that build your personalized skin profile (~2 min).
• Education: Not every skincare product a TikTok influencer posts matches any skin. 
  Using SkinQueens helps people discover if the product they saw in a TikTok video 
  actually matches their skin.
• Dupe Finder: Finds cheaper alternatives to expensive products with the same 
  active ingredients.
• Product Conflict Detection: Flags when products in your routine cancel each 
  other out.

4.8 stars · 10K+ Happy Queens
"Stop guessing. Start matching."
Download: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/skinqueen/id6761360693
```

### Content Requirements

**Platform**
```
Primary: TikTok
Secondary: Instagram Reels
```

**Video Specs**
```
• Format: Vertical 9:16
• Resolution: 1080x1920 minimum
• Duration: 15–30 seconds (sweet spot: 15–20s)
• Audio: Clean — no background noise, no music over voice
• Lighting: Natural — not over-produced
• Style: Authentic and casual — must look like organic content, not an ad
• App on screen: Minimum 3–5 seconds of actual in-app usage
```

**Quantity per Creator**
```
40–50 videos per month (~10–12 per week)
```

**DO's**
```
✅ Show the app being USED — not just the icon or splash screen
✅ App name (SkinQueens) visible on screen for at least 3 seconds
✅ Film genuine reactions to quiz results and product verdicts
✅ Use the Share-to-App flow as the core content moment
✅ Let the app speak for itself through screen recording
✅ Reply to comments asking "what app is this?"
```

**DON'Ts**
```
❌ NO direct CTAs — no "link in bio," no "download now," no "use my code"
❌ NO scripted or rehearsed-sounding reactions
❌ NO medical or dermatological claims of any kind
❌ NO specific skin result promises ("this will cure your acne")
❌ NO mention of competitors by name in a negative way
❌ NO over-produced, polished aesthetic — must feel native
❌ NO TikTok watermarks when repurposing to Instagram
```

### Hook Angles

**Tier 1 — Test first (highest conversion potential):**

```
1. Impulse Interception
   Hook: "POV: I almost bought this $80 serum until I shared the video to SkinQueens"
   Mechanic: Watch a viral skincare video → share to app → react to the verdict on camera.
   The screen-record of the share flow IS the content.

2. Waste / Drawer Audit
   Hook: "My skincare drawer is $400 of products I never use. This app told me why."
   Mechanic: Show your messy drawer → scan products one by one → react to the verdicts.

3. Product Conflict Reveal
   Hook: "Three products in my routine were canceling each other out"
   Mechanic: Enter your AM/PM routine → app flags conflicts → genuine reaction.

4. Skin DNA Quiz Reveal
   Hook: "Wait, my skin is actually THIS type?"
   Mechanic: Take the quiz on camera → read results out loud → react to your archetype.
```

**Tier 2 — Secondary angles:**

```
5. Dupe Finder: "This app found a $14 dupe for the $80 thing I was about to buy"
6. Anti-Influencer: "Stop buying skincare your favorite creator told you to buy"
7. Routine Integration: Show your routine + mention you checked everything here first
```
