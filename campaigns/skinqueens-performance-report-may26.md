## What's Working / Pattern Analysis

**Winning content formula (from top posts):**
1. **Emotional discovery hook** in first 1–2s ("She told me to use...", "This changed my skin")
2. **App visible on screen** — every qualifying post shows the SkinQueens UI
3. **Under 30 seconds** on TikTok, music added
4. **Authentic reaction** — not scripted, creator's real voice

**TikTok vs Instagram:** TikTok outperforms by 24% avg views. New dedicated accounts still building IG algorithm traction — expected to normalize in 2–3 weeks.

**Creator archetypes performing best:** Conversational skincare creators ("SkinTok Scroller" persona) who talk to camera, show reaction to app results. Tutorial-format creators are performing lower.
